Our Mission

The Problem

Suicide is a major issue present in our world currently. It takes the lives of thousands of people everyday, and often times it's because no one is there to help.

Causes

Those that consider suicide likely experience a deep feeling of hopelessness. Depression, the root of suicide is widely considered to be a key risk factor.

Friends

People facing depression typically tend to resort to their friends first when seeking assistance. Friends are people that they can trust and are comfortable with allowing for a safe environment.

Early Detection

It is very important that messages hinting at suicidal intentions are dectected right away. If noticed early, proper assistance can be provided in order to help those dealing with major depression.

Our Process

We have created an organized step by step plan for people to first detect if their friend is potentially suicidal, and then pair them with a therapist to help them with a therapist best suited for their problem.

Primary Diagnosis

Using our basic NLP model, a friend can either input a screenshot, or write out text and will be able to see if their friend is just suffering depression, or is on the suicidal side.

Progression Model

The friend will then input more data and our progression model will be able to detect what stage of depression the person is in and what could be the cause of it.

Locating Therapists

Once the proper diagnosis has been reached, our algorithms will be able to match a personalized therapist to the depressed friend.

Getting Support

The friend will then be able to visit the map embedded within the website to contact local therapists and create a plan in order to support the friend that is depressed.

Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

Don't people know if someone is suicidal?

Suicidal intentions are very hard to detect within a person. People would typically be quiet about it and wouldn't want to open up because they often feel lonely and hopeless. Our model will be able to look at previous suicidal posts and will compare them with the inputted text in order to give an accurate conclusion on whether or not your friend is actualy suicidal.

Who is this site specifically meant for?

This cite is mainly meant for friends of those who are going through depression so they can accurately see specifically what their friend is dealing with.

How do I use this site?

In order to maximize the use of this site, you will first have to input the message that your friend sent to you, wheter it is a screenshot of your chat or just typing it out. Our NLP model will then analyze the message, telling you if it signals suicidal intentions. Based on your results, you can then check out the map embedded within our cite, and then search for a therapist nearby and sign up through a forum to get in contact with them. Additionally, therapists can use this as a tool to gain a better understanding of their patients.

Why is our site better than existing models?

Unlike current existing models, which send an automated robot to help interact with the depressed person, ours is a full step by step process that will involve an experienced properly trained therapist and will eventually root back to the friend. This is important because the person that is depressed is much more likely to be more receptive to advice from their friends and real human beings rather than automated messages.
